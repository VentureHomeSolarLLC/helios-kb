## Active Financing Partners

### LightReach (Palmetto)
- **Type:** Solar Lease / PPA
- **Customer Support:** (855) 732-7282
- **Installer Portal:** [palmetto.com/installer](https://palmetto.com/installer)
- **Transfer Process:** Customer or installer initiates via Palmetto portal. Buyer must pass credit check.
- **Notes:** Lease vs. PPA depends on the state. Terms and billing are functionally identical for the customer.

### Sunnova
- **Type:** Solar Lease / PPA / Loan
- **Customer Support:** (866) 786-6682
- **Installer Portal:** [partner.sunnova.com](https://partner.sunnova.com)
- **Transfer Process:** Contact Sunnova to initiate transfer. Buyer credit check required.
- **Notes:** Some systems transitioned to SunStrong for warranty/service (see SunStrong transition FAQ).

### Enfin
- **Type:** TPO (Third Party Ownership)
- **Customer Support:** Contact via sales representative
- **Credit Requirements:**
  - FICO 620+ for auto-approval (TransUnion)
  - Below 700: stated income required (no W2)
  - Utility delinquencies checked via Experian (rarely an issue)
- **Notes:** See MEMORY.md entry from 2026-02-09 for detailed credit and utility bill requirements.

### GoodLeap
- **Type:** Solar Loan
- **Customer Support:** (844) 766-4663
- **Installer Portal:** [goodleap.com](https://goodleap.com)
- **Transfer Process:** Loan must be paid off at sale. Cannot be transferred to buyer.

### Mosaic
- **Type:** Solar Loan
- **Customer Support:** (855) 746-7849
- **Installer Portal:** [joinmosaic.com](https://joinmosaic.com)
- **Transfer Process:** Loan must be paid off at sale. Cannot be transferred to buyer.

## General Notes

- Always verify the financing type before advising a customer on transfers, payoffs, or modifications.
- For TPO/lease customers: Venture Home does NOT own the system. Defer ownership questions to the financing company.
- For loan customers: the homeowner owns the system but has a lien. Loan must be satisfied before clear title transfer.
- Document all financing-related customer interactions in Forge.
